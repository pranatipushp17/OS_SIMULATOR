About Process Scheduling

Process scheduling algorithms determine the order in which processes are executed by the CPU to optimize system performance and resource utilization.

FCFS: First Come First Serve
SJF: Shortest Job First
SRTF: Shortest Remaining Time First
Round Robin: Time Quantum Based
Priority: Based on Priority Values
